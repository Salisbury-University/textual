David Miličević (born 2 March 1992) is a Croatian handball player who currently plays for Zamet.[1]

He has competed in SEHA League, EHF Cup and EHF Cup Winners' Cup.

International career[edit]

Miličević for various national team youth selections. He played for Croatia U-21 at 2011 Youth World Championship where they finished in eight place[2] and 2013 Juniors World Championship where they lost in the third place match to France.[3]
