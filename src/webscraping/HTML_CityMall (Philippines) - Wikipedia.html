CityMall Commercial Centers, Inc. is a subsidiary of DoubleDragon Properties. It owns a chain of community shopping malls, with branches across the Philippines.[2]

History[edit]

The CityMall brand was launched in late 2015, with CityMall Arnaldo, Roxas as its first mall.

In November 2016, Grand City Mall Mandalagan, Bacolod made its grand opening, making it the first Grand City Mall to open.[3]
