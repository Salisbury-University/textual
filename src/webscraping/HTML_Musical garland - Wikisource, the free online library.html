Musical Garland
3179741Musical Garland
​

THE

MUSICAL

GARLAND:

CONTAINING

A CHOICE COLLECTION OF

Love, Patriotic, Comic, Naval, and Sentimental

SONGS.


MUSIC, 'TIS THINE TO CHARM THE SOUL,

AND ALL ITS HARSHER POWERS CONTROLL.



FALKIRK:

PRINTED AND SOLD BY T. JOHNSTON.
