Mount Angsi (Malay: Gunung Angsi) is a mountain which is located between the border between Ulu Bendul, Kuala Pilah District and Senawang, Seremban District in Negeri Sembilan, Malaysia. Part of the Titiwangsa Mountains, it is the sixth tallest mountain in the state, behind Mts. Besar Hantu, Berembun, Telapak Buruk, Datuk and Bintongan, with an elevation of 2,707 feet (825 m).[2]
