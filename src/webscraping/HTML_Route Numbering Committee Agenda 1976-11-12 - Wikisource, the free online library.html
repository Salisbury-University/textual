AMERICAN ASSOCIATION OF STATE HIGHWAY AND TRANSPORTATION OFFICIALS

341 National Press Building
Washington, D.C. 20045

ROUTE NUMBERING COMMITTEE AGENDA
November 12, 1976
Hyatt House
Birmingham, Alabama

 
STATE ROUTE DESCRIPTION
Arkansas U.S. 67

Relocation
Search
(Approved)

Beginning at the Intersection of present U.S. 67 at Bald Knob thence southwesterly over the new location to the intersection of U.S. 67 South of Search.
U.S. 67 C

Elimination
Judson
(Approved)

Eliminate as a U.S. Route present U.S. 67 C near Judson.
U.S. 79

Relocation
W. Memphis
(Approved)

Beginning at the Intersection of U.S. 70 and present U.S. 79 at Lehi thence west over U.S. 70 to Shearerville, thence South over a new facility to the intersection of present U.S. 79 at Chatfield.
U.S. 79

Relocation and Designation of a Business Route
Camden
(Approved)

Beginning at the Intersection of U.S. 79 and a newly constructed facility N.E. of Camden thence in a Southwesterly direction over the facility to the intersection of U.S. 79 in Camden. The old route to be designated as U.S. 79 Business.
Florida U.S. 98

Relocation and Designation of an Alternate Route
Panama City
(Approved)

Beginning at the intersection of U.S. 98 and U.S. 98 Bypass at St. Andrew Bay; redesignate present U.S. 98

and redesignate present U.S. 98 as U.S. 98 Alternate; to their intersection at Phillips Inlet.

Georgia U.S. Rt. 129

Marking of Temporary Rt.
(Approved)

Designate as temporary U.S. 129 Alternate former U.S. 129 between Hawkinsville and Macon. ​
Idaho U.S. 20

Relocation
Mt. Home
Resubmission
(Approved)

Beginning at the intersection of present U.S. 20 and S.R. 68 at Carey thence west over S.R. 68 through Fairfield to the intersection of present U. S. 20 at Mountain Home.

U.S. 93

Relocation and Elimination of an Alternate Route

 Redesignate present U.S. 93 Alternate as U.S. 93 between Challis and Shorshone and eliminate U.S. designation through Hailey, Ketchum, and Stanley.
Illinois U.S. 40 Business

Elimination
E. St. Louis
(Approved)

Eliminate as a U.S. Route present U.S. 40 Business between Maryville and East St. Louis
U.S. 150

Elimination
Rock Island
(Approved)

Eliminate as a U.S. Route present U.S. 150 between the intersection of U.S. 67 at Rock Island and U.S. 6 at Moline.
Kentucky U.S. 68

Recognition of a Bypass Route
Paris
(Approved as U.S. 68 and U.S. 68 Business)

Designate as U.S. 68 Bypass, a newly constructed facility at Paris.
U.S. 127

Relocation
(Approved)

Beginning at the intersection of U.S. 127 and a newly constructed facility north of Albany thence north over the new facility to the intersection of S.R. 90 thence west over S.R. 90 to the intersection of present U.S. 127.
Massachusetts-
New Hampshire 		U.S. 3

Relocation
Tyngsborough
{Approved)

Beginning at the intersection of present U.S. 3 and a newly constructed facility north of Nashua thence south over the new facility to the intersection of present U.S. 3 south of the New Hampshire state line.

Tyngaborough.

Nevada-Utah U.S. 50 Alternate

Removal
(Approved)

Remove as a U.S. Route present U.S. 50 Alternate between Moark Junction, Utah and East Ely, Nevada. ​
Nevada-Utah U.S. 50

Relocation
(Approved)

Eliminate present routing of U.S. between Green River Junction, Utah and Delta, Utah and sign U.S. 50 over I-70 from Green River Junction, Utah to Salina, thence west over S.R. 26 to Scipio, thence south over I-15 to Holden thence west over S.R. 26 to the intersection of present U.S. 50 in Delta, Utah.
U.S. 50 Alternate

Designation
Fernley

Designate as U.S. 50 Alternate the concurrent routing of U.S. 93 Alternate from Silver Springs, Nevada through Fernley to a junction with present U.S. 50 west of Fallon.
(Approved contingent upon removal of U.S. 95 Alternate between Fernley and Fallon)
U.S. 93 Alternate

Designation
(Approved as Temporary Alternate)

Designate as U.S. 93 Alternate the route from Wells, Nevada east overlapping I-80 to Wendover thence south over former U.S. 50 Alternate to present U.S. 93 South of Currie.
North Dakota-
South Dakota 		U.S. 81

Relocation
(Disapproved)

Beginning at the intersection of present U.S. 81 and I-29 east of Wahpeton, N.D. thence south over I-29 to a connection with present U.S. 81 north of Watertown.
Pennsylvania U.S. 422 Business

Relocation
New Castle
(Approved)

Beginning of the intersection of a newly reconstructed facility and present U.S. 422 Business in New Castle thence north west over the new facility to the intersection of U.S. 422.

Special Items

Missouri I-35

Relocation
(Approved)

Further consideration of additional information furnished at request of Subcommittee on November '75 submission.
Virginia-D.C. I-395

(Approved)

Confirmation of tentative approval of route number given in '75.
