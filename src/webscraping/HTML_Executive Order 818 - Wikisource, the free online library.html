Executive Order 818
by President of the United States
Authorizing Temporary Appointments Under Banking Act of 1908 in Treasury Department Without Regard to Civil Service Rules
◄  Executive Order 817
635936
Executive Order 819  ►
Signed by President Theodore Roosevelt Monday, June 15, 1908
1908 CSR 122
See Executive Order 741, January 27, 1908, and the Aldrich–Vreeland Act, May 30, 1908.
 more info.

The requirements under Civil Service Rule VIII regarding temporary appointments, as amended by executive order of January 27, 1908, are hereby waived as to temporary appointments in the Treasury Department under the act of Congress approved May 30, 1908, "To amend the national banking laws." Temporary appointments under the act above referred to may be made by the Secretary of the Treasury without regard to civil-service rules for such time as may be necessary to carry into effect the provisions of the act mentioned.


Theodore Roosevelt.

The White House,

June 15, 1908.
