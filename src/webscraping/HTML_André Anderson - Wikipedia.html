André Anthony Anderson (born May 1, 1988) is a former American football running back. He played his college football at Tulane.

Professional career[edit]

Anderson went undrafted in the 2010 NFL Draft. On April 24, Anderson signed a contract with the Buffalo Bills. He was cut by the Bills during final cuts on September 4, 2010 but was re-signed to their practice squad on September 21, 2010. On October 6, 2010, Anderson was promoted to the Bills' 53-man active roster.[1]
