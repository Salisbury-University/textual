​

The Honorabe Edward Shippen, Esquire, was appointed President of the Court of Common Pleas, for Philadelphia County, on the 7th day of May 1784.


Jonathan-Bayard Smith, Esquire, was appointed Prothonotary of the Court of Common Pleas, for Philadelphia County, on the 25th day of August 1777.
